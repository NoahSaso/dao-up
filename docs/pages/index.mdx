# DAO Up!

**DAO Up!** is a crowdfunding platform for DAOs.

Unlike traditional crowdfunding platforms DAO Up! guarentees that
backers become part of the DAO that they fund. In other words, when
you back a project on DAO Up! you also become part of that project and
its community.

Want to learn more about what a DAO is and what they can do? A great
place to start is Nick Merrill's
[substack](https://substack.com/profile/13257156-nick-merrill). Nick
is a member of DAO DAO and has some great explainers.
